Walk 2 - Viaduct City Walk



From the viaduct area there are many routes you can take in order to access the City Centre. The easiest way is to walk down Allergate, on to Crossgate, and over Framwellgate bridge onto Silver Street. Follow the page on to Google Maps in order to find a personalised route form your starting point.

Distance: 1-2 Miles

Grade: Easy

Route surface: Pavements and some hilly areas

If you have any queries about any of the routes feautured then don't hesitate to contact us through the form found on the Home Page
