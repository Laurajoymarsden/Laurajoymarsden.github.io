Walk 1 - Riverside Walk







This is one of our favourite walks down the river with some beautiful views of the cathedral from a secluded path. A good starting place is the steps down from Kingsgate Bridge next to Hatfield College which will take you down to the river side path. By walking right and staying on the path past Prebends Bridge, from which you can witness some beuatiful views. The path comes out by 9 Altars cafe on Silver Street, just down from the Market Square.

Distance: 1-3 Miles

Grade: Easy

Route surface: off road and gradual slopes
